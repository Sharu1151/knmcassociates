Corporate Services

At KNMC & Associates, we provide professional assistance to companies at every phase of their business life cycle. Our services span from initial incorporation, company law advisory, and statutory secretarial compliance, to strategic mergers and corporate restructurings.

Our corporate service wing is staffed by experienced advisors, chartered accountants, and company law consultants who manage the legal complexities on your behalf, ensuring seamless compliance with the Ministry of Corporate Affairs (MCA), SEBI, and other regulatory frameworks.

Our Specialized Solutions

Company Incorporation

Formation of Private Limited Companies, Public Companies, One Person Companies (OPC), Section 8 companies, and Limited Liability Partnerships (LLP).

Company Law Consultancy

Expert guidance on corporate governance regulations, provisions of the Companies Act, and board advisory support.

Mergers & Restructuring

End-to-end planning, due diligence, legal procedures, and strategic advisory for corporate mergers, acquisitions, and de-mergers.

Statutory MCA Filings

Preparation and filing of annual returns (MGT-7), financial statement forms (AOC-4), and various other event-based secretarial documents.

Clause 49 Compliance

Review and validation of corporate governance procedures to ensure complete compliance with corporate, listing, and tax laws.

Secretarial Operations

Handling share transfers, transmissions, allotments, capital changes, board resolutions, and drafting general meeting notices.

Statutory Registers

Systematic maintenance of mandatory register books, minute books of board and general meetings, and corporate file archives.

Equity Issues & Capital Structure

Consulting and compliance management for launching rights issues, issuing bonus shares, private placements, or public issues of shares.

Corporate Alterations

Managing alterations to the Memorandum & Articles of Association (MOA & AOA), including name changes, objects changes, and registered office shifts.

Audit & Assurance

At KNMC & Associates, we provide reliable and objective audit and assurance services that verify the integrity of your financial records. We translate raw numerical data into constructive management recommendations.

Our audit methodologies comply with applicable Indian and International Auditing Standards (IFRS) and statutory requirements. We evaluate your internal controls, identify operational gaps, and check expenditure genuineness to secure your corporate financial health.

Our Audit Methodology & Services

Systems & Control Study

In-depth study and analysis of existing systems, procedures, and internal controls to design suggestions for strengthening operational workflows.

Statutory & Policy Compliance

Ensuring complete compliance with corporate policies, institutional guidelines, and relevant state and central statutes.

Accounting Standards Review

Reviewing financial records to verify they align with Generally Accepted Accounting Principles (GAAP) and applicable Accounting Standards/IFRS.

Expense Verification

Checking the genuineness and authorization of operational, administrative, and capital expenditures booked in the accounts.

Operational Reporting

Reporting operational and process inefficiencies across all organizational departments, with constructive suggestions to resolve them.

Income Leakage Prevention

Detecting and preventing leakages of revenue or income and suggesting structural measures to block recurrence.

Financial Book Certification

Formal certification confirming that books of accounts are in exact agreement with the Balance Sheet and Profit and Loss account.

Statutory Audit Reports

Issuing formal audit reports under various corporate, direct tax, indirect tax, and regulatory compliance laws.

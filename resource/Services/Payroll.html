Payroll Services

At KNMC & Associates, we offer fully automated, compliant payroll management solutions for organizations of all sizes. Outsourcing your payroll to KNMC ensures absolute accuracy in salary computations and statutory filings.

Our payroll processing system manages salary disbursements, TDS computations, and provident fund contributions. Your employees receive automated, password-protected email payslips, and your organization remains fully aligned with labour and tax regulations.

Our Core Payroll Services

Salary Sheet Compilation

Compiling monthly salary sheets, calculating net payable components, and tracking attendance, leaves, and overtime registers.

Statutory Deductions

Calculating salary deductions in compliance with direct tax (TDS), Provident Fund (PF), ESI, and state Professional Tax (PT) rules.

Challan Deposit Services

Calculating, generating, and depositing statutory payroll contributions (TDS, PF, ESI) with appropriate central and state authorities.

Salary Disbursements

Coordinating online salary bank transfers and disbursements, generating bank upload formats for batch processing.

Secure Email Payslips

Generating and dispatching password-protected digital payslips directly to employee emails, ensuring maximum data confidentiality.

TDS & Withholding Tax

At KNMC & Associates, we provide comprehensive Tax Deducted at Source (TDS) and Tax Collected at Source (TCS) compliance management services. We ensure your business remains fully compliant with withholding tax regulations.

Our services cover monthly TDS computations, deposits, quarterly e-TDS return filings, correction statements, and representations during TDS assessments. Trust KNMC to manage your withholding tax pipeline accurately and on time.

Our Specialized TDS/TCS Solutions

TDS & TCS Compliance Advisory

Advising on withholding tax applicability rates, thresholds, and compliance requirements under direct tax laws.

TAN Procurement

Procuring Tax Deduction and Collection Account Numbers (TAN) for corporate, partnership, and individual deductors.

Withholding Tax Audits

Periodic review and audit of organizational expenses to verify accurate tax deduction rates and compliance compliance.

Monthly TDS Computations

Calculating monthly TDS liabilities on employee salaries, vendor contracts, professional fees, and interest payouts.

Monthly TDS Reconciliations

Reconciling tax deductions booked in the accounting ledgers against physical bank payments and online challan deposits.

Challan Deposit Management

Drafting TDS challans and managing the timely deposit of monthly taxes online with commercial banks.

TDS Certificates (Form 16/16A)

Generating and issuing digital TDS certificates (Form 16 for employees and Form 16A for vendors/parties) from the TRACES portal.

Quarterly e-TDS Returns

Structuring data and filing quarterly e-TDS returns (Form 24Q for salary, Form 26Q for non-salary, and Form 27Q for non-residents).

TDS Correction Statements

Preparing and filing correction statements to resolve PAN mismatches, challan errors, or default notices received from TRACES.

TDS Assessments Support

Representing your organization before the Income Tax officer (TDS) for scrutiny assessments, defaults, and interest dispute clearances.

Corporate Governance

At KNMC & Associates, we provide comprehensive governance consulting to build strong internal controls, transparency, and stakeholder trust. Sound corporate governance ensures organizational sustainability and ethical growth.

Our services cover board committee formation, independent reviews, transparency audits, and board meeting support. We help companies design corporate governance structures that comply with corporate law, listing requirements, and international best practices.

Our Governance Solutions

Periodic Internal Auditing

Designing and executing regular internal audits to monitor processes, detect discrepancies, and verify system efficiency.

Independent Audits

Conducting unbiased, objective financial and operations audits to give stakeholders assurance of account integrity.

Independent Verification

Rigorous third-party verification of key performance metrics, contracts, and financial asset disclosures.

Effective Supervision

Evaluating supervisory frameworks and reporting hierarchies to guarantee direct alignment with board-level directives.

Accountability Frameworks

Structuring accountability policies and role-based responsibilities to build trust and compliance across management tiers.

Board Composition Advisory

Ensuring a sufficient number of independent, qualified directors on your board to meet SEBI and MCA statutory mandates.

Audit Committee Formation

Setting up independent, qualified audit committees to oversee corporate reporting and internal audit operations.

Disclosure & Transparency

Promoting corporate transparency through adequate disclosure rules in annual reports, financials, and shareholder updates.

Board Meeting Participation

Procedural participation and secretarial assistance during board of directors and general body meetings.

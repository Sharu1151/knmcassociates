Indirect Tax / GST

At KNMC & Associates, we provide comprehensive Goods and Services Tax (GST) consultancy services to keep your operations compliant and tax structures optimized. We manage migrations, filing, refunds, and assessments under the GST Act.

Our tax consultants analyze business processes, reconcile Input Tax Credit (ITC), perform audits, and advise on transaction structuring. Trust KNMC to handle the operational details of GST compliance while you scale your business.

Our Comprehensive GST Solutions

GST Registration & Migration

Obtaining new GSTIN registrations, amending existing profiles, and managing tax migration protocols for all entity types.

GST Return Filing

Periodic preparation and filing of GST returns (GSTR-1, GSTR-3B, composition dealer GSTR-4, ISD, non-resident, and e-commerce returns).

GST Consultancy

Ongoing advisory on intricate legal tax issues, valuation rules, place of supply, tax rates, and classification of goods/services.

Business Segment Impact Analysis

Analyzing the direct impact of GST provisions on pricing strategies, working capital, raw material logistics, and operational margins.

GST Audits (Form 9C)

Conducting statutory reconciliations and annual GST audits in accordance with the provisions of the GST Act, 2017.

GST Implementation

Designing invoicing formats, mapping tax codes in accounting software, and training your teams on operational GST compliance.

Input Tax Credit (ITC) Management

Compiling input tax credit records and performing reconciliations (GSTR-2B vs. books) to ensure you claim optimal and eligible credits.

GST Assessments & Representation

Advising on state/central tax department notices, drafting replies, and representing clients in GST audit assessments and appeals.

Transitional Management

Assisting in managing the transition from legacy pre-GST taxes (VAT, Service Tax, Excise) and claiming opening credit balances.

Records Compliance

Assisting in maintaining structured digital records, sales/purchase registers, tax invoices, and credit/debit notes required by law.

Strategic Tax Planning

Developing long-term supply chain and contract structures to legally minimize your overall indirect tax burden.

Vendor/Buyer Coordination

Coordinating vendor reconciliations to identify non-compliant suppliers who have failed to deposit GST, securing your input tax credit eligibility.

GSTN Number Verification

Due diligence services verifying the validity, filing frequency, and structural compliance profile of business partner GSTIN numbers.

HSN Code Classification

Assisting in finding and verifying the correct Harmonized System Nomenclature (HSN) and SAC codes to prevent invoicing discrepancies.

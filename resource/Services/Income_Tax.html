Income Tax / Direct Tax

At KNMC & Associates, we provide comprehensive direct tax consultancy services to individuals, partners, and corporate firms. We aim to minimize your tax liability through effective, legal, and forward-looking tax management and compliance strategies.

Our direct taxation division manages tax planning, return filing, and salary structures. We also offer expert representation in direct tax assessments, litigation, appeals, DTAA treaties, and FEMA regulatory compliance.

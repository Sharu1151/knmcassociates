Services for Non-Residents

At KNMC & Associates, we provide comprehensive financial, direct tax, and regulatory compliance services to Non-Resident Indians (NRIs), Overseas Citizens of India (OCIs), and foreign corporate entities.

Our specialized team handles investment advisory, direct tax filing, repatriation audits (Form 15CB/15CA), and representations before the Reserve Bank of India (RBI) under FEMA guidelines, helping you navigate cross-border regulatory frameworks smoothly.

Our NRI & Non-Resident Solutions

Non-Resident PAN Procurement

Acquiring Permanent Account Numbers (PAN) for individual NRIs, foreign citizens, and overseas business entities holding Indian investments.

NRI Tax Planning

Structuring investment profiles and assets legally in India to optimize direct tax burdens and capitalize on DTAA benefits.

Advance Tax Rulings

Obtaining formal Advance Rulings from tax authorities on complex or debatable direct tax questions related to foreign transactions.

FEMA & RBI Consultancy

Comprehensive advice on foreign exchange rules, outward remittances, and capital transactions under the Foreign Exchange Management Act.

Non-Resident Tax Filings

Preparation and online filing of annual Income Tax returns for NRIs and foreign companies earning taxable income in India.

Indian Investment Advisory

Assisting in structuring investments in Indian mutual funds, shares, government bonds, or start-up capital.

Repatriation Certifications

Auditing and issuing Form 15CA and 15CB certifications required by banks for outward repatriation of funds and asset sale proceeds.

RBI Property Transaction filings

Handling applications to the RBI for acquisition, lease, purchase, or sale of residential and commercial properties in India by non-residents.
